About DGSP

What is DGSP?

The Digital Government Service Platform is India's unified digital gateway, designed to simplify access to every central and state government service. From Aadhaar to agriculture, from PAN to public health — DGSP brings it all under one roof.

With AI-powered assistance, step-by-step guidance, and multi-language support, DGSP ensures no citizen is left behind in India's digital transformation journey.
